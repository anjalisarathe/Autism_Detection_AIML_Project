Understanding Autism

Autism is a spectrum disorder characterized by unique strengths and challenges. Our mission is to educate and support early intervention through innovative tools and reliable information.

Explore our About page for more detailed insights, or try our screening tool to get started.

Moreover, autism encompasses a diverse range of behaviors and skills.
