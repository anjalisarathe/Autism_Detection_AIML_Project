About Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a diverse and complex neurodevelopmental condition that affects the way individuals perceive, communicate, and interact with the world. It manifests uniquely in each person, resulting in a rich tapestry of abilities and challenges. From remarkable attention to detail and creative problem-solving to difficulties in social communication, every individual on the spectrum is truly one-of-a-kind.

Early detection, compassionate intervention, and consistent support can empower those on the spectrum to harness their unique strengths and lead fulfilling lives.
